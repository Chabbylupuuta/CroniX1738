About Us

17 March . 2024

CroniX: More than just history. This isn't just any magazine – it's a revolution. The CroniX E-Magazine is Zambia's first premier student-run magazine, by students, for students.

Born from student initiative, CroniX is here to empower its rightful owners: the Zambian student body. We're taking back the narrative and creating a platform that truly speaks to you.

We're more than just a magazine. We're a passionate team of writers, editors, publicists, poets, accountants, and even the occasional legal whiz. But most importantly, we're programmers who believe in the power of technology to connect and empower.

Unveiling the CroniX Experience

CroniX is an ever-evolving platform. We encourage you to read, engage, share, and most importantly, connect with us! We're open to anything – advertisements, collaborations, or even just saying hello. Let's build something amazing together.
